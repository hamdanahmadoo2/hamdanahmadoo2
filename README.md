## 🤖 Generative AI & Machine Learning Mastery

My expertise spans the entire lifecycle of Generative AI, moving from foundational text models into the cutting edge of multi-modal systems.

### 🌐 Large Language Models (LLMs) & Fine-Tuning
* **Architecture Deep Dives:** Extensive hands-on experience with the inner workings of **BERT, BART, T5, GPT, DeepSeek, and LLaMA**.
* **Custom Fine-Tuning:** Specializing in Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT) and LoRA to adapt massive models to highly specific business domains without insane compute costs.
* **The "From Scratch" Project:** Successfully engineered a complete Transformer architecture from the ground up to deeply understand self-attention mechanisms and positional encoding.

### 🔍 Advanced RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
* Moving beyond basic vector search. I implement advanced RAG concepts including:
  * Semantic chunking and metadata filtering.
  * Re-ranking algorithms to ensure maximum context relevance.
  * Multi-query generation and hybrid search (Keyword + Vector).

### ⚙️ Autonomous AI Agents & Workflows
* Utilizing **LangChain** and custom Python/Node.js logic to build multi-agent systems.
* Designing workflows where AI can execute API calls, scrape the web, read databases, and make autonomous decisions to automate complex pipelines.

### 🎨 Multi-Modal & Image AI (Current Focus)
* Transitioning from pure text to comprehensive multi-modal systems.
* Working with image-generation pipelines, vision-language models (VLMs), and building applications that can understand, process, and generate both text and visual data simultaneously.
